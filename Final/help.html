Help & Support

1. How can I create an account?

To create an account, simply click on the "Sign Up" button at the top right corner and follow the instructions. You'll need to provide your email and set a password.

2. How can I reset my password?

If you forget your password, go to the login page and click "Forgot Password." Enter your email, and you'll receive a link to reset your password.

3. How do I update my account information?

You can update your account details by logging in and navigating to the "Account Settings" section. From there, you can change your personal information, email, or password.

4. How do I contact customer support?

You can reach out to our customer support team by filling out the contact form on the "Contact Us" page or by emailing us directly at support@medicalservice.com.
