General Information
About Us

City Health Hospital is a leading healthcare institution, known for its world-class infrastructure and top medical professionals. Established in 1985, we have been offering the best healthcare services in the region. Our specialties include Cardiology, Oncology, Neurology, Orthopedics, and more. We aim to provide holistic care for our patients, with state-of-the-art equipment and highly trained staff.

Services We Offer
Emergency Care

Our emergency department operates 24/7, providing critical care and advanced life-saving procedures in a timely manner.

Outpatient Department (OPD)

Our OPD offers consultations across various specialties. Patients can book appointments with ease and receive timely care.

Surgical Services

Our surgical team offers cutting-edge procedures, ensuring the best possible outcomes for both minor and major surgeries.

Our Facilities
Laboratory

Our fully-equipped laboratory offers diagnostic services including blood tests, biopsies, and imaging scans.

Pharmacy

We have an in-house pharmacy that provides prescription and over-the-counter medications 24/7.

Intensive Care Unit (ICU)

Our ICU offers round-the-clock care for critically ill patients, with the latest in life-support systems and monitoring.
